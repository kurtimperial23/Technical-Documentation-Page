CATS

Cats are believed to be the only mammals who don't taste sweetness. Cats are nearsighted, but their peripheral vision and night vision are much better than that of humans. Cats are supposed to have 18 toes (five toes on each front paw; four toes on each back paw). Cats can jump up to six times their length.

DOGS

Dogs are one of the most beloved and popular pets in the world. They are domesticated mammals that are often kept as pets or used for various purposes such as hunting, herding, guarding, and assisting people with disabilities. Dogs come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and colors, and can range from small lap dogs to large working breeds.

BIRDS

Birds are fascinating creatures that come in a wide range of sizes, shapes, and colors. They are members of the class Aves and are characterized by their feathers, beaks, and wings. Birds are found in virtually every environment on earth, from tropical rainforests to Arctic tundras, and they play a vital role in the ecosystems they inhabit.

SNAKES

Snakes are a diverse group of reptiles that are found on every continent except Antarctica. They come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and colors, and range from small, harmless species to large, venomous predators. Snakes are characterized by their long, slender bodies and lack of legs, which allows them to move in a unique serpentine motion.
